# Venue Scope

Use this reference when the literature survey asks for CCF-A or high-quality venue filtering, formal publication status, or papers from venues such as FSE, ASE, ICSE, ISSTA, TSE, TOSEM, OOPSLA, PLDI, or related SE/PL/AI venues.

## Primary Software Engineering Venues

Prioritize formal papers from:

- FSE / ESEC/FSE
- ASE
- ICSE
- ISSTA
- TSE
- TOSEM
- OOPSLA
- PLDI
- POPL
- OSDI

## Related SE/PL Venues

Include when topic-relevant:

- NDSS
- S&P
- JSS
- ECOOP
- ICPC
- ICSME
- MSR
- SANER

## AI/NLP Venues For LLM4SE

Include when the paper is directly relevant to LLM4SE:

- NeurIPS
- ICML
- ICLR
- ACL
- EMNLP
- NAACL
- AAAI
- IJCAI

## Publication Status Rule

Do not infer formal publication status from arXiv, OpenAlex, Semantic Scholar, Google Scholar, or general search results alone.

Confirm formal publication status using authoritative sources such as:

- DBLP
- ACM Digital Library
- IEEE Xplore
- Springer
- official conference proceedings
- official journal pages
- official conference websites
- official artifact or proceedings repositories

When a paper is only available as an arXiv/preprint, technical report, workshop paper, or unpublished repository, label it clearly and do not present it as a confirmed formal CCF-A publication.
